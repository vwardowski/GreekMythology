General Information
Zeus

Zeus is the king of Mount Olympus, where all of the olympians live. He is the god of lightning, storms, and the sky. He is apart of most greek myths.

Hera

Hera is the wife of Zeus. She is the goddess of marrige and she has to deal with Zeus having affairs with other woman often and this compels to cause trouble.

Hermes

He is the messenger god. He helps dieliver messages from the gods, and sends people to the underworld. He is one of the few gods/people that have been to the underworld, and have been able to come back.

Aphridite

She is the goddess of love and beauty. She is married to Hephaestus, but is in love with Ares, which causes many problems.

Hades

He is the king of the underworld and does not like to be referred to as an olympian. He rarely visits Mount Olympus and spends most of his time in the underworld.

Poseidon

He is the god of the sea. He created horese and cyclops, which is very important in the story The odessy

Ares

He is the god of war. The amazons, warrior women, were his daughters. He was never very popular among mortals (because he is the god of war) so not many people worshipped him.

Athena

She is the god of wisdom/war strategy (and weaving). The city athens is named after her because of a competition with Poseidon, they were always enemies. She created spiders, and is one of the virgin goddesses.

Artemis

She is the goddes of the hunt and the twin of Apollo. The Hunt is a group of immortal women who pledge their allegence to her and "hunt" monsters to protect the gods (and humans). She is one of the most competative gods, and is known for creating nymphs.

Apollo

He is the sun god, and drives the sun charriot, which makes the sun rise each morning. He is also the god of music, art and medicine, and is knon as the "healer"

Demeter

She is the goddess of Harvest. She is the mother of persephone, and is responsable for seasons to express the pain of losing her daughter.

Dionysus

He is the god of wine, and was origionaly mortal/has mortal parents. He is responsible for a constalation in the sky. Also he is the last god to enter Olympus.

Hephaestus

He is the god of work and fire. He makes most of the weapons that the gods use. His marrige with Aphridite was an arranged marrige to prevent war.

Persephone

She is the goddess of flowers and the Queen of the Underworld. She is the wife of Hades, and is directly respnsible for the creation of seasons.
